About

This website is an independent, non-governmental initiative focused on sharing readable, source-backed information about Mukundara Hills Tiger Reserve (MHTR).

The intent is not promotion, tourism, or advocacy for any single outcome, but public understanding. Content here is drawn from official documents, peer-reviewed research, and long-form studies, and is presented in a way that is accessible without oversimplifying ecological or conservation realities.

This site does not publish sensitive wildlife locations, live movement information, or precise spatial data that could place species or habitats at risk.

Scope & boundaries

  • Independent and unaffiliated with any government department
  • No commercial tourism content or endorsements
  • No real-time wildlife sightings or sensitive geospatial data
  • Claims linked to primary sources wherever possible

Why this exists

Mukundara Hills Tiger Reserve is often discussed through narrow or fragmented lenses. This initiative attempts to place landscape, biodiversity, and conservation decisions in a broader ecological and social context, without noise or sensationalism.

Any errors or omissions are unintentional. Corrections or additional references are welcome.
